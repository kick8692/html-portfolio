About Me

Hello, I am a 19 year Army veteran. I have had three combat tours and three overseas assignments during my career. I enlisted in the Army from Kansas City, MO at the age of 17. My first Military Occupational Specialty (MOS) was Transportation Management Coordinator (88N). I obtained the rank of Sergeant First Class (E-7) before commissioning as a Warrant Officer. My current MOS is Mobility Officer (882A). I am currently stationed at Fort Liberty, NC with my wife, daughter, and three boys.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and have completed multiple coding bootcamps from Python, Java Script, and I am currently working on a Web Development bootcamp. This website is my class project for my latest course!

I am also a photographer. Photography has been a hobby of mine since I was in highschool. I have experience taking a performing post processing for social media and businesses. I enjoy street, nature, and arcitecural photography. My favorite subjects are my kids!
