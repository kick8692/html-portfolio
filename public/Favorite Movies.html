The Best Movies According to Sean

My top three movies of all-time.

  1. Star Wars - A New Hope

    This was my grandpa's favorite movie and he told me he saw it twice in theatres on the same night it came out!

  2. Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade

    Of all the Indiana Jones movies, I find this one to be the best... followed by Raiders of the Lost Ark

  3. Gladiator

    My name is Maximus Decimus Meridius, commander of the Armies of the North, General of the Felix Legions and loyal servant to the true emperor, Marcus Aurelius. Father to a murdered son. Husband to a murdered wife. And I will have my vengeance, in this life or the next. - Maximus Decimus Meridius (Gladiator)
